Empowering Individuals Through Marathon Training

What is the main purpose of this marathon event?

The main purpose of this marathon is to raise awareness and funds for educational programs that empower children and promote playful learning experiences. We aim to support initiatives that provide quality education and foster creativity and innovation in young minds.

How can I participate in the marathon?

You can participate by registering on our website. Simply click on the 'Register' button, fill in the required details, and choose your participation category. We offer various categories to accommodate different age groups and fitness levels.

Where and when will the marathon take place?
